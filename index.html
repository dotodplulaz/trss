1. COMPANY BACKGROUND INFORMATION (TRSS)

The organization is carrying out various activities and renders services without any discrimination to sex, religion, tribe, belief, nationality, or political affiliation in the society.


In making sure that, our vision are fulfil, the organization has been engaged with committed staffs who are helping the socities and driven organization for future generation.

The “Tanzania Rehabilitation and Social Support” is wishing to impact and change peoples’ lives to standard level of living. Also, influencing people not to lose hope but to “MOVE ON” and claim their place and space in all spheres of life through information, empowerment, skills development and providing the necessary resources and mentorship.

Tanzania Rehabilitation and Social Support is a newly established Non Profit Non-Governmental organization (NGO Reg. Nr: ooNGO/R/3384) based in Arusha, Tanzania. Our focus is to enhance development of the people in all spheres; economically socially and environmental sustainably. At Tanzania Rehabilitation and Social Support, the main key of the organization is to assist people of all categories in providing a sustainable economy, better health, and better life in a conducive environment.

Organization Membership

Tanzania Rehabilitation and Social Support acknowledges that it cannot operate in isolation. On account of it is still looking/inviting for membership/partnership who can participate and work together to improve the standard of living to the people
